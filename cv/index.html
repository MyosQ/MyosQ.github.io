My programming journey started in C when I was entertaining myself with Project Euler. The programming was then very mathematically oriented. I got very confident in C and started to use it for more general programming, such as Unix system programming. Slowly, I moved on to Python, which is today my main language. I am very confident with all its libraries and specail features, and I have used it for a wide range of applications, such as web development, scripting in general and machine learning. Having played with websites throughout the years, I have also inevitably learnt Javascript. Today, I have a lot of experience with both front-end and back-end development in Javascript. I recently started learning Rust, but do not have any professional experience with it yet.
